About Me

I am self-taught illustrator with passion in 2D movies. Being raised in a family of artists, I began drawing at young age and quickly became fascinated with capturing real-life objects and emotions through art. Inspired by traditional animation, I strive to bring characters and scenes to life with creativity, depth and feeling.
